About the Treasure Hunt

Every year my family ventures forth to the beach for holiday weekends like countless other families. For me, the most important thing is making sure that even with the hustle of daily life, my wife and I set aside quality time for our little ones. Setting aside a full week is a luxury that I don’t take lightly. The hustle of our daily lives won’t matter much in 5 or 10 years, but the memories from trips like this will last a lifetime. When I was younger, I was excited about the possibility of one day going on a

treasure hunt.💰
I remember a story from grade school where the teacher talked about a modern day discovery of a shipwreck and the myth of nearby plundered gold from a Spanish galleon. From there, my imagination took off. I pictured myself as a young adventurer, searching for hidden treasures on a remote island.
